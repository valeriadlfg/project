Facts about Squirrels

What do Squirrels eat?

As we can see, Squirrels eat lots of nuts and seeds.

Where do Squirrels live?

Squirrels usually nest outside in tree trunks but in climates where the weather gets cold, they will seek refuge inside a home, shed or garage.

How many species of squirrels are there in the world?

More than 200 species live all over the world, with the notable exception of Australia.
